The Cheetah

The cheetah is the fastest mammal in the world, they can accelerate from 0 to 68 miles per hour in just three seconds.

  • Scientific Name:Acinonyx jubatus
  • Habitat: Grasslands, savannahs (the majority of cheetahs live on natural reserves or parks.)
  • Average length(without tail): 3.6-4.6ft
  • Average lifespan: 10-12 years

Just before they gain full speed, cheetahs use their keen eyesight to scan the area for signs of prey. This big cat hunts during the day, with their distinctive coat they can easily blend into the high, dry grasses.
